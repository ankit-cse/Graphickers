Mock-ups

In manufacturing and design, a mockup, or mock-up, is a scale or full-size model of a design or device, used for teaching, demonstration, design evaluation, promotion, and other purposes When we are working on graphic design projects we could need a mockup on different occasions.

Just to make a final example – think about printing design projects: we can’t print everything, for sure, just to show the proposal to the client. We can’t print in offset, or digital-offset, but it’s not so easy to print everything with our office inkjet printer too.

In these cases we can use flat design proposals – PDF, for instance – to show the global project, but using mockups can add some advantages since the client better understands the final result.
